\# Home Assistant - Snapmaker Camera Keepalive



A Home Assistant add-on that keeps the Snapmaker U1 camera stream awake by periodically calling the Moonraker websocket `camera.start\_monitor` method.



\## Installation



1\. In Home Assistant, go to \*\*Settings → Apps → App Store\*\*.

2\. Open the three-dot menu.

3\. Select \*\*Repositories\*\* or \*\*Add App Repository\*\*.

4\. Add:



```text

https://github.com/evansmike881/Home-Assistant---Snapmaker-Camera-Keepalive
